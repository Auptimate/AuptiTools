Data Sources

i

Where every number comes from.

  • Deals file — open pipeline & every closed (won/lost) deal (name, product, target month, total value, weighted value, stage).
  • Billed file — reconciled invoiced revenue by client, product, month (USD).
  • Quota file — monthly sales target.
  • Plan file (optional) — Product | Deals | Value Each. If present it drives the rest-of-year plan and the recommended actions; otherwise the built-in plan is used. Product names must match the list in Definitions.
  • Refresh: the dashboard fetches all sheets from a single read-only Apps Script feed on every page load and re-applies the rules in the browser — edit a sheet, reload, done. The badge at the top reports whether you are seeing live data or the built-in snapshot fallback, and when the sheets were last edited. Every figure traces to a row.
08 · Reference

Definitions

i

What each term and formula means.

Open pipeline
Weighted value of open-stage deals (Discovery → Account Activated) in months that haven't closed yet. Excludes Closed Won / Lost.
Coverage
open pipeline / remaining quota.
Benchmark coverage (3×)
Industry "healthy" rule of thumb — implicitly assumes 1-in-3 converts.
Required coverage
1 / win rate — the coverage you need given your actual conversion. Moves with win rate.
Win rate
Closed Won ÷ (Won + Lost) across every decided deal, by count or value. Per product; n = sample size. Updates as deals close.
Deals needed
plan wins / win rate — pipeline volume implied to hit the plan.
MQL → SAL → SQL
Funnel stages: marketing lead → sales-accepted → sales-qualified (opportunity, i.e. becomes pipeline).
Lead-to-close
Back out leads from wins: wins / win rate / SAL→SQL / MQL→SAL. Upper rates are editable benchmarks.
Monthly fill
Open pipeline in a month vs that month's quota. Face = weighted; Adjusted = total × win rate.
Attainment
billed / quota.
Billed / Pipe mix
Share of billed revenue / open pipeline by value.
Mix signals
Build billed>pipe · Validate pipe>billed · Cliff billing, no pipeline.
Projected FY
billed-to-date + open pipeline × factor (worst / base / best).
09 · Reference

Assumptions & Method

i

The judgment calls behind the numbers.

  • Pipeline = open stages only (Discovery, Deal Qualified, Account Activation, Account Activated); Closed Won & Closed Lost excluded. Elapsed months carry no pipeline — the closed/open split is derived from today's date, not hardcoded.
  • Product mapping: Holding SPV → Custom Service, Fund → Fund Admin. Nova and Nova+ tracked together (no separate Nova+ history).
  • Win rates from every decided deal in the Deals sheet, so they re-rate automatically as deals close (no fixed window). Products with n<3 fall back to the blended rate; n is shown next to each so thin samples are visible.
  • Monthly fill, two lenses: Face = weighted pipeline; Adjusted = total value × win rate (applied to total, not weighted, to avoid double-discounting).
  • Projection: Worst seeded from the per-product win rate; Base editable; Best = 100%. "Locked" = invoiced.
  • Lead-to-close funnel: SQL → Win uses your win rate; MQL → SAL (40%) and SAL → SQL (50%) are industry benchmarks (editable), since upstream marketing-funnel data isn't tracked yet — same status as the 3× coverage rule. Assumes leads convert within the plan window; a longer sales cycle pulls the lead-gen earlier.
  • Currency: all USD. Watch — Arcanys: Closed Won $70K weighted, $30K billed; $40K won-but-unbilled not counted yet.
